Our Best Breeds

Golden Retriever

The Golden Retriever is a sturdy, muscular dog of medium size, famous for the dense, lustrous coat of gold that gives the breed its name.

Corgi

The Pembroke Welsh Corgi is a strong, athletic, and lively little herder who is affectionate and companionable without being needy. They are the most agreeable breed of all small housedogs.

Poodle

The Poodles are extremely intelligent and are easily trained. They are agile graceful, and smart. Poodle also excel in variety of canine sports that includes agility, obedience, and tracking.

Husky

The Siberian Husky, a thickly coated, compact sled dog of medium size and great endurance. It was developed to work in packs, pulling light loads at moderate speeds over vast frozen expanses.

Pug

The Pug has been bred to be a companion and a pleasure to his owners. They are known for being even, stable temperament, great charm, loving, and outgoing personality.
